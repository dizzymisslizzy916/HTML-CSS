Monthly Events:

Art, cheese, and wine (First Saturday of the Month, 2 pm – 5 pm)

Join us for an exhibition of local or visiting artists with a glass of wine. The price of $8.00 per person (must be 21 or older) includes a glass of wine (red or white) and a plate of cheese and fruit samples. The art works are available for purchase.

Special events:

Star Trek Cosplay Month: October 1, 2021 – October 31, 2021

Celebrating the iconic series, patrons will be amazed with a series of themed drinks and food offerings. Cosplays and panel discussions on Friday evenings throughout the month. Ticket and drink passes are available in advance. See your barista for details!

Concord High School Art Project: May 11, 2021

Newark High School students present their art works at this annual event. The official opening of the exhibition starts at 4 pm. The art works will be exhibited through the end of June.
